Background

In 2015 I became interested in web design, such as creating layouts in programs like Photoshop. This didn't last long, before I dropped out of college. In 2017 I persued game design. I tinkered around languages such as C# and Java for many years, creating all sorts of fun things. I didn't feel it was the right path for me, given how saturated the market was at the time, and the immense difficulty of a solo game developer.

Web design had always been there in the back of my mind, but I enjoyed coding in my spare time too. I made the decision to become a frontend web developer full-time, combining the skills I enjoy, design and function.
